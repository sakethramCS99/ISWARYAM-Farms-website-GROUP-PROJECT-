Experience Sustainable Agriculture

Join us for an enriching corporate visit to Iswaryam Farms, where you will immerse yourself in the world of sustainable agriculture and organic farming. Our visit will provide a comprehensive tour of our lush fields, showcasing our innovative farming practices and commitment to environmental stewardship. You will have the opportunity to participate in hands-on activities, such as planting and harvesting, while learning about the technology and methods we use to produce high-quality organic crops. Enjoy a farm-to-table lunch featuring fresh produce from our fields, and engage in insightful discussions with our experts on the importance of sustainability in agriculture. This visit is not only a chance to learn and network but also an inspiration to incorporate sustainable practices into your own business or community. Come and experience the harmony of nature and innovation at Iswaryam Farms.

Community Engagement

Friends from various apartment communities have enjoyed an enriching and memorable visit to Iswaryam Farms. The attendees immersed themselves in the serene farm environment, exploring our lush fields and witnessing our sustainable farming practices firsthand. They engaged in hands-on activities such as planting and harvesting, gaining practical insights into organic agriculture. The visit included a delightful farm-to-table lunch, featuring fresh produce straight from our fields, which everyone enjoyed immensely. The day was filled with insightful discussions on sustainability, allowing guests to learn how these practices can be applied in their own communities. This visit not only provided a unique educational experience but also fostered a sense of community and connection with nature, leaving everyone inspired and informed.

Rotary Club's Insight into Sustainable Agriculture

Members of the Rotary Club recently visited Iswaryam Farms, where they had the opportunity to delve into the world of sustainable agriculture. The visit offered a comprehensive tour of our organic farm, showcasing our innovative farming techniques and commitment to environmental stewardship. Rotary Club members actively participated in farm activities such as planting and composting, gaining hands-on experience and understanding the importance of sustainable practices. They also enjoyed a wholesome farm-to-table lunch, prepared with the freshest ingredients from our fields. The day included insightful discussions on the impact of organic farming on community health and environmental sustainability. This visit provided Rotary Club members with valuable knowledge and inspiration to support sustainable initiatives in their own projects and communities.

Educational Adventure: Discovering Sustainable Farming

We invite schools to bring their students to Iswaryam Farms for an educational and inspiring visit. Students will have the opportunity to explore our vibrant fields and learn about sustainable and organic farming practices in a hands-on environment. During the visit, they can participate in activities such as planting seeds, harvesting crops, and understanding the importance of composting. The experience is designed to educate young minds about the farm-to-table journey, the significance of environmental stewardship, and the benefits of healthy eating. A delicious lunch made from fresh, organic produce grown on the farm will be served, providing a tangible connection between the fields and their meals. This visit aims to spark curiosity, foster a love for nature, and promote sustainable practices among the younger generation, making it a memorable and impactful educational outing.
