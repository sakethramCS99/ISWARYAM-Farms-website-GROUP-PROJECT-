Greenhouse Farming in Tamil Nadu

Greenhouse farming at Iswaryam Farm involves the sustainable cultivation of various crops in controlled environments to ensure high-quality produce and ecological balance.

Greenhouse Preparation

Iswaryam Farm designs greenhouses to optimize air circulation and sunlight exposure, crucial for plant health and growth.
Prioritizing clean water through natural filtration methods and regular monitoring to maintain optimal conditions for plants.

Planting and Nutrition

Careful selection of crop species suited to local climate and greenhouse conditions to maximize growth and yield.
Balanced feeding regimes using natural fertilizers and supplements to enhance plant growth and health.
Regular testing and adjustments to maintain ideal pH levels and soil conditions for optimal plant growth.

Sustainable Practices

Using natural fertilizers and minimizing chemical inputs to promote a healthy greenhouse ecosystem.
Optimal use of resources like water and nutrients to minimize waste and environmental impact.
